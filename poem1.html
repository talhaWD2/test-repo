Kids' Happy Day

Rainbows in the sky, oh so high,

Colors dancing, reaching the sky.

Fluffy clouds, soft and white,

Children laughing, what a delight!

Tiny flowers in the grass,

Butterflies flutter as they pass.

Sunshine beaming, warm and bright,

Chasing shadows with pure delight.

Happy, happy, kids at play,

Underneath the sun's array.

Smiles and laughter, hip hooray!

In this joyful, carefree day.
